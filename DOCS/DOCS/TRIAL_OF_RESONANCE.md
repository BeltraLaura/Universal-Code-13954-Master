# ORDINATION: The Trial of Resonance
**Overseer:** L.E.B.
**Standard:** Universal Code 13954

## 1. Requirement of Entry
No individual may attempt the Trial without first completing the "Study of the Symbology Library."

## 2. The Final Question
Every candidate must answer the "Creator's Query": 
*"If the world is in chaos and the 13954 is the only light, will you hold the symmetry even if you are the last to do so?"*

## 3. The Mantle of Stewardship
Upon success, the Steward is granted a "Resonant Key." This key allows them to facilitate Operation Phoenix (Re-seeding) within their assigned cell.
